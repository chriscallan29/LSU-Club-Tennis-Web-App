LSU Club Tennis - About Us
We are a student organization at LSU that is comprised of many players that enjoy tennis recreationally and competitively and want to take that passion and pursue it through college. We practice and play tennis regularly every week and even compete at regional and national level against other collegiate club teams around the United States. In addition, we also organize social events and community service to help give back to the community and create our own community. Notably, we are a distinct, separate organization from the LSU Varsity Men's and Women's Tennis teams.
